About Me:

Hey there! I'm a rising junior at Rutgers University, majoring in Computer Science and Data Science with a minor in Mathematics. Why pick one brain-bending field when you can tackle three? I finished high school in India and then took a giant leap (and a long flight) to the U.S. It was a tough decision, but it turned out great! Now, I spend my days coding, analyzing data, and solving math problems – all while searching for my lost coffee.
